Fast Food Pack - Low Poly Series.
==============================

This package contains 24 ready-to-use low poly models of 12 kinds of fast food.

List of Assets

- Burger (492-932 tris)
  Variation: plain, cheese, tomato & cheese, big

- Chicken Nuggets (364 tris)

- Churros (240 tris)
  Variation: plain, choco, strawberry

- cup (240-391 tris)
  Variation: coffee, soft drink

- Frenchfries (232 tris)

- Fried Chicken (110 tris)

- Hot Dog (534 tris)

- Onion Rings (488 tris)

- Pizza (155 tris)

- Sandwiches (230-513 tris)
  Variation: egg, fruits, strawberry, tomato

- Soft Serve (152 tris)
  Variation: choco, green tea, milk, strawberry

- tacos (374 tris)


Optimized for

- PC
- Mobile
- VR


All models are in .fbx format.
All models use Only one material (1024 x 1024 texture in .png format).
All objects are optimized to have as few triangles as possible without losing quality and style.
